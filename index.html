About Collegiate Overwatch

Collegiate Overwatch is where the best college eSports players compete in North America.

Colleges around the United States, Mexico, and Canada get their best players together to compete in tournaments. This year the Overwatch Collegiate Homecoming will run for 2 months with teams competing every weekend for a spot in the championship. With $27,600 dollars on the line, the teams take it quite seriously. Does your school have what it takes to win it all?

This year the Overwatch team is working with JMU X-Labs to create an official team. Thanks to this partner we now have a space where we can practice and compete together in the same room while on campus. With the help from X-Labs, the goal is to create an eSports program at JMU that rivals other big names in the state.

JMU Overwatch is Powered by X-Labs
