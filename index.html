About Collegiate Overwatch

Collegiate Overwatch is where the best college e-sports players compete in North America. Colleges around the United States, Mexico, and Canada get their best players together to compete in tournaments. This year the Overwatch Collegiate Homecoming will run for 2 months with teams competing every weekend for a spot in the championship. With $27,600 dollars on the line, the teams take it quite seriously. Does your school have what it takes to win it all?
